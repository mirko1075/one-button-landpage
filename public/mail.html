How it works

1. Tap once. InOneButton listens to your meeting in real time.
Important: audio is not recorded or saved — it is only processed live.

2. Real-time transcription. Words appear instantly on your screen, then disappear when the meeting ends.
We do not store transcriptions unless you choose to save summaries.

3. Automations run. After the meeting ends, summaries and action items are generated and delivered to your email or Slack.
No bots. No setup. No friction.

As soon as your access is ready, you’ll receive:

  • Your private invitation link
  • Setup page to choose your delivery method (Email / Slack)
  • Access to the one-tap real-time transcription interface
